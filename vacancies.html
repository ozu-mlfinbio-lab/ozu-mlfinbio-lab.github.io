Vacancies

We accept MSc and PhD applications from good candidates who have strong programming skills and a solid mathematical background. Prior courses in machine learning, deep learning, bioinformatics, or finance are highly beneficial.

Application Instructions

Please include the following documents:

  • CV
  • Transcripts
  • Short research statement
  • Publication record (if applicable)

Send the application to emre.sefer[at]ozyegin.edu.tr

Additionally, ask two references to directly email recommendation letters in support of your application.
